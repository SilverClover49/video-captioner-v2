Video Captioner

Vision-Based AI Pipeline for Silent Video Captioning

Track 2: Video Captioning | Gemma 4 31B | 100% Vision-Based

01

The Problem

"Most captioning tools rely on audio. But what about silent videos?"

  • Audio-based pipelines fail completely on silent videos
  • No visual understanding of the scene
  • Can't describe objects, colors, setting, or mood
  • Track 2 requires describing what's IN the video, not what's said
02

Our Solution: 5-Stage Vision Pipeline

Stage 1
Frame Extraction
→
Stage 2
Vision Analysis
→
Stage 3
Frame Comparison
→
Stage 4
Thinking Board
→
Stage 5
Caption + Verify

Key Innovation

  • Adaptive frame count (9-15 based on duration)
  • Gemma 4 31B vision analysis
  • Frame-to-frame comparison
  • Per-video thinking board

Quality Assurance

  • Auto-scoring (1-10 per caption)
  • Re-generation for scores below 8
  • Style-specific verification
  • Complete output guarantee
